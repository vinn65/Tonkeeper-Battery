Introducing Tonkeeper Battery

Effortless Transactions, No Gas Fees

Tonkeeper Battery is a revolutionary new feature that eliminates the need to acquire and hold TON cryptocurrency to pay for gas fees on the TON blockchain.

With Tonkeeper Battery, you can receive, send, and swap tokens like stablecoins, Notcoin, and NFTs without having to worry about gas fees. The battery automatically covers the transaction costs, allowing you to focus on your crypto activities.

Key Use Cases

Gasless Payments and Swaps

Tonkeeper Battery enables you to make payments and swaps for USDT, Notcoin, and other tokens without the need to purchase TON cryptocurrency. The battery covers the gas fees, so you can transact seamlessly.

Effortless NFT Transfers

With Tonkeeper Battery, you can transfer your NFTs without worrying about the gas fees. The battery automatically handles the transaction costs, making it a breeze to move your digital collectibles.

Funding Options

You can easily top up your Tonkeeper Battery using in-app purchases or by sending TON or supported stablecoins (currently jUSDT) to a special battery.ton address. This ensures your battery is always charged and ready for your transactions.
